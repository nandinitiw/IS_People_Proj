Budget by semester and week—by college students, for college students.

Problem Solution Strategy Impact Team

Money shows up in chunks. Bills do too.

We built a simple web app idea that maps aid, paychecks, rent, and weekly spending on one timeline—so you see what’s left before midterms, not after.

Irregular income Big semester costs Weekly pace

1 The opportunity

Who: Students paying their own way + families & schools who care about stability.

  • Income jumps (aid, paychecks)—not a steady monthly check.
  • Tuition, rent, books hit in waves.
  • Typical apps assume “divide by 12.” Semesters don’t work that way.

Core idea: It’s a tooling mismatch—not laziness.

2 Our system

Lightweight website + simple data store. You type amounts; the app does semester math and gentle warnings.

3 Strategy

Value

Turns messy inputs into time-aware guidance—decision support, not just a spreadsheet.

Chain

Capture → semester math → dashboard & alerts → you tweak the plan.

Edge

Built for aid cycles & lumpy bills—generic budgeting apps aren’t.

Scale

Digital, partner-friendly (clubs, aid office). Room for campus data later.

Wins for students (less stress), schools (retention support), families (fewer surprises).

4 Impact

Organizations

Shift from only crisis help to earlier, self-serve planning.

Society

Young adults practice pacing money when stakes are real but smaller.
